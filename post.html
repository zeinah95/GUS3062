Best Three Maps that I made in GUS 3062

Posted by Zeinah A. on December 12, 2023

Lab 3: Spatial Mapping of US Census Data

Lab Objectives

This lab aimed to describe the spatial distribution of the Hispanic population in the contiguous U.S. by state, according to the 2010 U.S. Census.

I started off by identifying states with the largest and smallest Hispanic population in the US 2010 Census dataset. I subsequently created a choropleth map that shows the percentage of Hispanics in each state.

Skills Acquired

Layout

Symbology

Map element use and placement

Data field identification and use

Manage and display map elements including legend, scale bar, title, and north arrow

Analyze and assess Census data in Arc GIS Pro

Create professional looking maps

Lab 4: Locations of Homocides in Chicago

Lab Objectives

The goal of this lab was to describe the spatial distribution of the density of homicides by police district in Chicago.

In order to accomplish this, I created a choropleth map of the density of homicides (per square mile) in Chicago by police district.

Skills Acquired

Creating Summary Tables

Calculating Areas

Joins and Relates

Field Calculators
